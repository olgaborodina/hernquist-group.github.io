Welcome!

Our group focuses on theoretical and computational studies of dynamical processes in cosmology and galaxy formation and evolution. We are a welcoming community covering a vast range of astrophysical scales and applications with state-of-the-art simulations.

Lars Hernquist

Mallinckrodt Professor of Astrophysics

Lars Hernquist is a world expert in cosmological simulations of galaxy formation and evolution. He has been involved in several large projects, including Illustris, IllustrisTNG, and MilleniumTNG. Lars is primarily interested in the role of stellar and AGN feedback in shaping the present day population of galaxies. Today, he is proud to facilitate an active and socially vibrant group.
